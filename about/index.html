Hi, I'm Gabriel Jaldon

This blog mainly covers topics on web development with Elixir and is meant to help others better appreciate this relatively new language. I've mainly used Ruby and Ruby on Rails in the past, but have found Elixir even more enjoyable to work with. I hope it goes the same for you!

If you are interested in web development with Elixir, follow me on Twitter where I will announce my latest articles. I'd be glad to hear from you too for any feedback and suggestions you might have.
